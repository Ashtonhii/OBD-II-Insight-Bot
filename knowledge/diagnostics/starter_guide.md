# Vehicle Diagnostics Starter Knowledge

## P0300 Random/Multiple Cylinder Misfire
- Typical symptoms: rough idle, hesitation, poor fuel economy, flashing MIL.
- Common causes: worn spark plugs/coils, vacuum leaks, low fuel pressure, injector issues.
- First checks:
  1. Inspect freeze-frame data and misfire counters by cylinder.
  2. Check ignition components and plug condition.
  3. Verify fuel trims for lean/rich conditions.

## P0420 Catalyst System Efficiency Below Threshold (Bank 1)
- Typical symptoms: MIL on, reduced fuel economy.
- Common causes: aged catalyst, upstream/downstream O2 sensor drift, exhaust leaks.
- First checks:
  1. Verify no active misfire or fuel control faults before catalyst diagnosis.
  2. Inspect O2 sensor switching behavior.
  3. Check for exhaust leaks ahead of the catalyst.

## High Engine Coolant Temperature
- Indicators: coolant temp steadily above normal range, fans running frequently.
- Common causes: low coolant level, thermostat stuck closed, radiator airflow issues, water pump degradation.
- First checks:
  1. Confirm coolant level and pressure test system.
  2. Command and verify cooling fan operation.
  3. Compare upper/lower radiator hose temps to assess thermostat flow.

## General Diagnostic Workflow
1. Verify customer complaint and collect DTC/freeze-frame data.
2. Prioritize root-cause codes over secondary/reactive codes.
3. Validate repair with drive cycle and confirm monitors complete.
